How to Use EduHub AET

1. Courses

Browse study materials organized by Level (1-4) and Semester. Each semester card shows credits, hours, and departmental options. Click "Access Materials" to open the Google Drive folder for that semester.

2. Class Routine

View the official class schedule for Level-4 Semester-2 (July-December 2024). Filter by your department (FPM, FSEE, IWM).

3. Community Q&A

Ask academic questions, share knowledge, and discuss topics with your classmates — like a mini-LinkedIn for AET students. You can post, comment, and react to discussions.

4. Photo Gallery

Browse and share photos from university events, field trips, lab sessions, and campus life. Upload your own photos and react with emoji or comment on others' photos.

Photo uploads require approval from admin unless you're an admin.

5. Study Abroad

Explore higher study opportunities at universities in Germany, USA, Japan, Australia, and more. Filter by country and find scholarships specifically for Agricultural Engineering students.

6. Study Diary

Your personal private notes space. Write, save, and download your study notes. Data is saved locally on your device.

7. Update Profile

Update your nickname, department, email, phone, hometown, hall name, and supervisor. You can also change your password here.

8. Night / Day Mode

Toggle between dark (night) and light (day) themes using the button at the top.

Your Data is Safe

All user data is stored securely in Firebase. Existing registrations will never be deleted. Contact admin at deboneel1998@gmail.com for any issues.
